# The Founder Universe — User Zero & the Forkable Startup (2026-07-09)

**Status:** Binding design note + seeding plan. The founder operates TinyAssets AS a universe; that universe, published, becomes the **Founder archetype** — a forkable startup-in-a-box any founder can run their own company on.

## 1. User zero seeds every side
The founder is simultaneously: **supply** (own rig on the curve at reserve 0; own printer taking fab jobs), **demand** (his standing goals: gardener, briefs, payables for the company's own books), **commons** (the six archetypes, Dataset Forge, adaptation profiles, the carrier), **bounties** (founder-funded seed board = the first public demand signal), and **operations** (below). Every seeded artifact is real usage, not fixtures — the receipts are marketing.

## 2. The company runs as standing goals (all existing machinery)
- **Engineering:** OpenSpec change proposals as goals; dev daemons as nodes; gates = tests/reviews (already how this bundle was built).
- **Public surfaces:** the WEBSITE IS A LENS — generated from the graph (commons artifact pages per discovery-flows + brain-crawl format), agent-readable (llms.txt/AGENTS.md), regenerable, never hand-rotted. Marketing = the self-marketing archetype (deck -> voice -> videos -> publish under action caps).
- **Fundraising:** investor CRM as a standing goal (pipeline tracking, follow-ups via message effectors under action caps); the deck/video pipeline feeds it. COMPLIANCE FLAG: the archetype ships PROCESS, not legal advice — securities-marketing rules vary; counsel gate applies to any forked use.
- **Finance:** the payables archetype runs the company's own books; treasury ops per token-architecture (legal-gated).
- **Hardware play:** Track I flows (carrier, cookbook, shuttle quotes) as goals with gate ladders.
- **Community:** bounty seeding, bug triage (write_page effector once P1 lands), attribution-claim outreach.

## 3. Publish it: the Founder archetype
The whole operating universe — scrubbed of secrets (grants/credentials never publish; scope promotion strips them by construction) — ships to the commons as the flagship archetype: **raise capital · build & maintain a platform · run a hardware play · operate every public surface.** Any founder forks it; the vibe-coder-with-a-startup-idea persona (demo §Act flow) gets not just a product path but a COMPANY path. Group scope (commons-architecture §3) is where a forked founder universe becomes a team.

## 4. Why this is the flagship
It is the dogfooding thesis at maximum: the strongest possible proof the platform works is that the company selling it RUNS ON IT — and the strongest possible commons artifact is the working shape of a company. "This startup is a fork" becomes a sentence real companies say. Marketing copy earned, not written: every pitch video, every paid maker, every Thursday payables run of TinyAssets itself is a live demo of the archetype.
